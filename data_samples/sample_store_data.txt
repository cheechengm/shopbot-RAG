SHIPPING POLICY
---------------

Standard Shipping:
- Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
- Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days within the continental US.
- Standard shipping costs $5.99 for orders under $50.
- FREE standard shipping on all orders over $50.

Express Shipping:
- Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $12.99.
- Same-day delivery is available in select cities for $19.99.

International Shipping:
- We ship to over 30 countries worldwide.
- International orders take 10-20 business days.
- International shipping starts at $24.99 and varies by destination.
- Customers are responsible for any customs or import duties.

Order Tracking:
- Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number.
- You can track your order at shopmart.com/track or using the carrier's website.
- Tracking information is typically available within 24 hours of shipment.

---

RETURN & REFUND POLICY
-----------------------

Return Window:
- We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date.
- Items must be in their original condition, unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached.
- Final sale items (marked with a red tag) cannot be returned or exchanged.

How to Return:
1. Log in to your account and go to "Order History"
2. Select the order and click "Request Return"
3. Print the prepaid return shipping label (for US orders)
4. Drop off your package at any UPS location within 7 days

Refunds:
- Once we receive your return, processing takes 3-5 business days.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed.
- Credit card refunds may take 5-10 additional business days to appear on your statement.

Exchanges:
- To exchange an item for a different size or color, please initiate a return and place a new order.
- We do not process direct exchanges at this time.

Damaged or Defective Items:
- If you receive a damaged or defective item, contact us within 7 days at support@shopmart.com.
- Include your order number and photos of the damage.
- We will send a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
---------------------------

Q: Can I change or cancel my order?
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Please contact support immediately if you need to cancel.

Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?
A: Yes! Gift wrapping is available for $4.99 per item during checkout. You can also include a personalized gift message at no extra cost.

Q: What is your price match policy?
A: We will match any competitor's current price on identical in-stock items. Submit a price match request via email with a link to the competitor's listing. Price matching does not apply to auction sites, clearance items, or marketplace sellers.

Q: How do I use a promo code?
A: Enter your promo code in the "Discount Code" field during checkout. Only one promo code can be used per order. Promo codes cannot be combined with other offers.

Q: Do you have a loyalty program?
A: Yes, our ShopMart Rewards program lets you earn points on every purchase. Sign up for free in your account settings.

Q: What if my package is lost or stolen?
A: If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you haven't received the package, wait 24 hours and check with neighbors. If still missing, contact us within 7 days and we will file a claim with the carrier and send a replacement or refund.

Q: Can I return a gift?
A: Yes. Gift recipients can return items for store credit without needing the original receipt. Contact support with the order number or the gift giver's email address.

Q: Is my personal information safe?
A: Yes. We never sell your personal data. Please read our Privacy Policy at shopmart.com/privacy for full details.
